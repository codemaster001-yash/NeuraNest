Instructions

Movement:
← Left Arrow and Right Arrow → keys (Desktop)
Left/Right buttons (Mobile)

Jetpack:
Press SPACEBAR (Desktop)
Tap anywhere on the game area or use the JETPACK button (Mobile) to fly upwards.

Obstacles:
Avoid colliding with the ground or flying blocks.
Collisions cost a life.
Lose all lives and it's game over!

Coins:
Collect yellow coins to increase your score.

Game Speed:
The game gets faster every 100 points you score!

Pause:
Press ENTER to pause/resume the game (Desktop).
